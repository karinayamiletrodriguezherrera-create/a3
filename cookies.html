Cookies Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains how Dynamic Fashion uses cookies and tracking technologies when you access our digital fashion atelier. By browsing our site, you consent to our configuration of cookies as outlined below.

1. What Are Web Cookies?

Cookies are small text records placed on your client browser when you visit a website. They serve to recognize user devices, facilitate correct page loading, remember preference variables, and analyze marketing traffic.

2. Cookie Classifications Employed

We deploy cookies under the following structural categories:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for basic system operations, navigation grids, checkout security, and session integrity. The website will not operate correctly without these.
  • Performance & Analytics: Aggregate anonymous metrics on page load speeds, popular runway capsules, and interface interactions (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Targeting & Advertising: Used by ad networks (such as Google Ads) to deliver relevant ad placements based on your previous browsing history.

3. Managing Cookie Preferences

You can choose to accept or reject cookies. When you visit our website for the first time, our Cookie Consent Banner allows you to select your settings. You can also modify your browser controls to decline cookies.
