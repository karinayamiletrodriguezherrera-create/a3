Sustainable High-Fashion Textiles

By Materials Scientist • 8 Min Read

Fast fashion relies on petroleum-derived polyester that sheds microplastics into oceans and breaks down after a few wears. **Sustainable High-Fashion Textiles** utilize GOTS certified 100% organic extra-long staple cotton and recycled ocean plastic nylons. This guide explores eco-textile innovation.

1. 480 GSM GOTS Organic French Terry

Organically grown without toxic pesticides or synthetic fertilizers, extra-long staple organic cotton fibers are loop-knitted into dense 480 GSM fleece that holds shape for decades.

2. 3-Layer Recycled Tech Nylon Membranes

Recovered ocean plastic fishing nets are re-polymerized into durable waterproof outer shells with 20,000mm hydrostatic head protection.
