Caring for Designer Streetwear

By Atelier Care Specialist • 8 Min Read

Washing heavy organic cotton hoodies in hot water with harsh detergents cause fabric fading and seam distortion. **Designer Streetwear Maintenance** preserves 480 GSM French terry loops, electric cyan piping, and magnetic closures. This guide details proper garment care.

1. Cold Water Gentle Wash

Turn heavy hoodies inside out and wash in cold water (30°C / 86°F) using mild liquid detergent. Never use fabric softeners that break down French terry loops.

2. Flat Drying & Magnetic Closure Protection

Reshape heavy hoodies while damp and dry flat on a clean towel away from direct sunlight. Ensure magnetic clasps are snapped shut before washing.
